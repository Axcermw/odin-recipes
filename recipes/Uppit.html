Uppit

Description

A 5-minute south Indian dish, also called Upma.
Add grean peas and carrot and make this into a quick nutritious meal. Had with a side of gun powder!

Ingrediants

  • 1/2 cup Uppit rava/ wheat rava
  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • 1/4 tsp Mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp Chana dal
  • 1/2 tsp Urad dal
  • 4 Curry leaves
  • 2 Green chillies (slit)
  • 1 Onion (chopped)
  • 1/4 tsp Fresh/frozen peas
  • 1.5 cups Water
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 tsp Sugar (optional)
  • 1/2 Lemon
  • 1 tbsp Coriander leaves (chopped)

Preperation Steps

  1. Roast uppit rava on medium low heat for 5 minutes. Keep aside to cool.
  2. Heat a thick bottomed pan on medium high heat. Add 1 tbsp oil.
  3. Add 1/4 tsp mustard seeds. Whwn mustard seeds starts to pop add 1/2 tsp chana dal, 1/2 tsp urad dal, 4 curry leaves, 1/4 tsp asafoetida (hing) and 2 slit green chillies.
  4. Fry 2 minutes.
  5. Add chopped onion and fry for 5 minutes. No need to brown the onions.
  6. When onion turns soft add 1/4 cup fresh or frozen peas and fry for 2 minutes.
  7. Add 1.5 cups of water, 1 tsp salt and 1/4 tsp sugar(optional). Bring to a rolling boil.
  8. When water starts to boil rapidly add roasted rava and keep stirring continuously until all the water is absorbed completely.
  9. At this point reduce the heat to lowest setting and simmer for about 2 minutes.
  10. Turn off the heat and add juice of half a lemon and 1 tbsp coriander leaves.
  11. Serve with gun powder!
